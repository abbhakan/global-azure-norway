Global Azure 2022 is a virtual conference taking place from May 5-7.
This is a community driven iniative and last year there were over 250 speakers from all around the world delivering some 300 sessions.
This year we at NNUG are hosting a stream for selected speakers covering various areas of Azure.

This page contains details on the sessions, speakers and contains a complete schedule.

The details and sign-up for each day are hosted on NNUG Oslo's Meetup pages.
